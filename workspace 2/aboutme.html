About Me

Hi there, Glazenettes. I'm Lex Mal, creater and founder of Glaze. I have always loved makeup growing up, from playing dress up with my mom to getting ready for prom. I didn't realize that this was my passion until my early twenties when I picked up a brush and everything had change. Fastforward to present where I own my own company. Sometimes I still can't believe it guys.

Glaze was born, raised and made with love in the city that never sleeps, NEW YORK. I wanted to create something for the everday girl but with the hustle and bustle that is my city. The concept for my beauty baby came from my love for food, so I thought it would be fantastic to name all my products after food. I mean who doesn't love food, am I right.They’re also, like, really pretty.

Trends come and go, so it’s super One of our top priority is to keep you up with this ever-changing industry without breaking the bank. We’re dedicated to creating the best possible products in every shade imaginable for our loyal custies.
